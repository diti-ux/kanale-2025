Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying GOONER tokens

There's no official minimum, but you'll need enough SOL to cover transaction fees (usually 0.001-0.01 SOL). Most users start with 0.1 SOL or more for a meaningful purchase.

Common reasons include: insufficient SOL for fees, slippage too low, network congestion, or incorrect contract address. Try increasing slippage to 2-3% and ensure you have enough SOL for fees.

Most wallets automatically detect new tokens. If not, manually add the token using the contract address: FP9axFNBC1V68BLTDqtWAP6vVVqP9daNr57wmx4Tpump. Look for "Add Token" or "Import Token" in your wallet settings.

For GOONER, we recommend 1-3% slippage. Start with 1% and increase if the transaction fails. Higher slippage means you might get fewer tokens but higher success rate.

Always verify the official contract address and only use reputable DEXs. Memecoin investments are high-risk. Never invest more than you can afford to lose, and always do your own research.

Solana transactions typically complete in 10-30 seconds. During high network activity, it might take longer. You can track your transaction using the transaction hash on Solscan.io.
